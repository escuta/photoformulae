Warm tone print developer

Water 125F/52C								750ml
Metol									1.6g
Sodium Sulfite - anhydrous (Sulfito de Sódio anidro)			24g
Hydroquinone (Hidroquinona)						6.6g
Sodium Carbonate - monohydrate* (Carbonato de sódio monohidratado)	24g
Potassium Bromide (Brometo De Potassio)	     	   			2.8g
Cold water to make	      						<1000ml
Mixing Instructions: Add chemicals in specified sequence.
Dilution: 1+1
Usage: Starting point time: 2 mins.

Notes 
*multiply amount by 0.833 if using Sodium Carbonate in anhydrous form.

Double the amount of Potassium Bromide to increase warmth. Increase dilution for more soft-working results.

If warming a 1 litre working solution, add 1.4g of Potassium Bromide.
