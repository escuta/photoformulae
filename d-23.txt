D-23 Formula (1 litre):

    700ml Water at 52°C
    Pinch of the Sodium Sulfite below 
    Metol – 7.5g
    Sodium Sulfite – 100g
    Water to Make 1L


D-23 Formula (750ml - used after discarding 750ml of old stock):

    525ml Water at 52°C
    Pinch of the Sodium Sulfite below 
    Metol – 5.6g
    Sodium Sulfite – 75g
    Water to Make 750ml
